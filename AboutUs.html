SaffCoZ | Apple Bloosom

Apple Blossom by SaffCoZ radiates elegance and freshness, inspired by blooming apple orchards. It opens with crisp green apples and juicy pear, leading to a delicate floral heart of apple blossom and jasmine. The scent settles into a warm, comforting base of amber and soft musk. Perfect for those seeking a balanced, uplifting fragrance, Apple Blossom is ideal for both everyday wear and special occasions, capturing the purity of nature with a modern, refined touch.

SaffCoz | Pear Bloosom

Pear Blossom by SaffCoZ exudes gentle sophistication and natural freshness, inspired by the delicate allure of blooming pear orchards. It begins with a bright and juicy blend of ripe pears and citrus, transitions into a soft floral heart of pear blossom and lily of the valley, and finishes with a warm, creamy base of sandalwood and white musk. Ideal for those seeking a graceful, uplifting fragrance, Pear Blossom is perfect for both daily wear and special moments, embodying nature’s charm with a contemporary elegance.

SaffCoz | Jus De Kiwi

Jus de Kiwi by SaffCoZ captures the zesty freshness and vibrant energy of a sun-kissed tropical paradise. It opens with a refreshing burst of juicy kiwi and tangy lime, followed by a playful heart of green tea and white florals that add a touch of elegance. The scent is anchored by a smooth base of vanilla and soft musk, leaving a light yet irresistible trail. Perfect for those who love a fruity, invigorating fragrance with a modern twist, Jus de Kiwi is ideal for everyday wear.

SaffCoz | Gummy Apple

Gummy Apple by SaffCoZ is a playful and sweet fragrance that evokes the joy of your favorite childhood treats. It opens with a burst of candied green apple and zesty lemon, leading to a heart of juicy red fruits and delicate peony. The base lingers with a warm blend of vanilla and soft caramel, adding a cozy, delectable finish. Perfect for those who love a fun and fruity scent, Gummy Apple is a delightful choice for casual outings and everyday wear.

SaffCoz | The Matcha

The Matcha by SaffCoZ is a serene and refreshing fragrance inspired by the timeless elegance of Japanese tea ceremonies. It opens with a crisp blend of green tea and bergamot, awakening the senses with its gentle zest. At its heart, creamy matcha mingles with delicate jasmine petals, creating a harmonious and soothing aroma. The base features soft musk and a hint of cedarwood, leaving a calming, earthy trail. Perfect for those who appreciate understated sophistication, The Matcha is ideal for daily wear or moments of quiet reflection.
